Doggy Rescue have many dogs that are in need of urgent help and wishes to get a new and warmly home.

Why adopt a dog?

There are a lot of abonned dogs that is currently homeless and living on the street in Spain. They do not have any good looking chances of surving on their own. All of the misunfortuned dogs are searching and hoping to get a better future and a safe home to stay in.

There are so many dogs that really needs urgent help, so instead of contribute to dog breeders and buy puppies, rescue an existing dog that really wishes to be your new best friend. To adopt a rescued dog will make you:

  • A happier person.
  • Find a new best friend.
  • Contribute for a better and more caring world.
  • Giving a poor dog a second chance and saving their life.
