About - Doggy Rescue

Est. 2006

Our story

Doggy Rescue was founded in Nerja, Spain in 2006 by Joana Ramirez and Elsa Goméz.

The need for a Dog Rescue Center was incredible important at that time and it is still to this day. Elsa and Joana was helping and taking care of a lot of dogs even before the Doggy Rescue was created. One day they had enough, there were just to many dogs that needed urgent help and medical treatment. Then the briliant duo came up with an idea and went strait into the majors office in the small town named Nerja. They said that it was about time that someone did something to help all this poor dogs. The mayor loved the idea and executed on it immediately. He promised to fund the whole project and only a few months later the Doggy Rescue Center was born. Yay!


Facility

Our facility and center is located in an old local renowated sugar factory close to Nerja town. Were there is room for over 500 dogs, a vetrinary reception and a large garden for the dogs to get sun, fresh air and be able to play.


Directions

  1. From Malaga airport, take the A-7 highway east going towards Almeria.

  2. After 40 minutes on the A-7 highway you take the road exit to Nerja.

  3. When you arrive at the first roundabout after the A-7, then take left and follow the street named Calle Manuel.

  4. After 5 minutes drive on Calle Manuel you will arrive at our facility.
